# 第二大脑

**分类:** 国际版补充  
**编号:** #09

---

## 🎯 痛点

- 记忆有限：读过的文章、学过的知识容易忘记
- 检索困难：想要用时找不到之前的信息
- 知识孤岛：不同笔记软件中信息无法关联
- 输出困难：积累了大量素材但难以转化为内容

---

## ⚡ 核心功能

1. **智能收集** - 自动保存有价值的内容（文章、对话、灵感）
2. **结构化存储** - 用双向链接建立知识关联
3. **语义检索** - 用自然语言搜索笔记库
4. **定期回顾** - 定时提醒回顾旧笔记，加深记忆
5. **内容生成** - 基于知识库生成文章、总结、回答

---

## 🛠️ 所需技能

- 飞书文档/多维表格 - 知识库存储
- `feishu_wiki_space` - 知识库管理
- `web_fetch` - 内容抓取
- `feishu_search_doc_wiki` - 语义搜索
- Cron jobs - 定期回顾提醒

---

## 📝 Prompt 模板

```
You are my second brain. Help me capture and retrieve knowledge:

1. When I share a link/article, extract and save:
   - Key insights (3-5 bullet points)
   - Source and date
   - My takeaways
   - Related existing notes (search for connections)

2. When I ask "what did I read about [topic]?", search my knowledge base and summarize:
   - Key points I've saved
   - Sources and dates
   - Actionable takeaways

3. Every Saturday, surface "old but gold" notes:
   - Pick 3 notes from 3+ months ago
   - Summarize their key ideas
   - Suggest how they might be relevant now

4. Help me create by asking:
   - "Based on my notes about [topic], what would a blog post cover?"

Keep everything in my [知识库名称] knowledge base.
```
